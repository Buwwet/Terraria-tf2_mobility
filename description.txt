TF2 Movement has some movement related weapons and accessories.

Weapons:
    Rocket Jumper: Shoots "harmless" knockback rockets that any soldier could love. Learn Rocketjumping!

Accessories:
    Thermal Thruster: Simple movement accessory concept. Jump and as you fall, gain thrust.

NOTE: There is no way to obtain these items in normal progression, they are for fun and experimentation only. You will need Cheat Sheet
